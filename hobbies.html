Culinary Exploration

Cooking is where science meets art, and I find tremendous joy in exploring new flavors and techniques. The kitchen becomes a laboratory of sorts ‚Äî experimenting with ingredients, understanding chemical reactions, and creating something that brings pleasure and nourishment to others.

I love the creative freedom that cooking provides, from traditional Persian dishes that connect me to my heritage, to international cuisines that broaden my culinary horizons. Each recipe is an experiment, each meal a small celebration of culture, chemistry, and creativity.

The precision required in cooking parallels the meticulousness needed in medical practice ‚Äî measuring, timing, adjusting, and perfecting. Yet it also offers spontaneity and the freedom to innovate, making it the perfect counterbalance to structured academic work.

Travel & Nature

Travel feeds my curiosity and expands my perspective beyond the laboratory and clinic. Exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, and immersing myself in nature provides a refreshing counterpoint to the intensity of medical studies.

Nature, in particular, offers profound moments of clarity and peace. Whether hiking through mountain trails, walking along coastal paths, or simply observing the intricate patterns in natural landscapes, I find inspiration in the complexity and beauty of the natural world.

These adventures remind me that medicine and science exist to serve humanity and preserve the precious experiences that make life meaningful. Every journey teaches empathy, adaptability, and appreciation for the diverse ways people live and thrive around the world.
