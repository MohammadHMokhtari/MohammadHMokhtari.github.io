About Me

Introduction

I am currently completing my Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) at Islamic Azad University in Isfahan, Iran. My passion lies in understanding and advancing cancer immunology, particularly immune-checkpoint modulation and novel therapeutic strategies.

Through my academic journey, I've developed a deep fascination with the intricate mechanisms that govern our immune system's response to cancer. My work focuses on bridging fundamental research with clinical applications, always keeping the human element at the forefront of scientific discovery.
